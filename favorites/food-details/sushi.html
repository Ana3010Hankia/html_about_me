Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. It is known for its fresh flavors, beautiful presentation, and variety of styles.

Why I Like This Food

I enjoy sushi because it’s light yet satisfying, fresh, and full of delicate flavors. It’s a meal that feels special and artistic, making every bite an experience.

What I Like About It

I love the variety sushi offers — from the silky texture of sashimi to the crunch of tempura rolls. The balance of rice, fish, and condiments like soy sauce and wasabi creates a perfect harmony of taste.
