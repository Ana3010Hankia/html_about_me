Butter Chicken

Why I Love Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is the perfect comfort food for me. The rich, creamy tomato-based sauce combined with tender chicken pieces creates an explosion of flavors that never gets old.

What Makes It Special

  • Perfect balance of spices - not too hot, not too mild
  • Creamy texture that's incredibly satisfying
  • Goes perfectly with basmati rice or naan bread
  • Reminds me of cozy dinner nights

Best Places I've Had It

While I've enjoyed butter chicken at many restaurants, the best ones always have that perfect balance of sweetness and spice, with chicken that melts in your mouth.
