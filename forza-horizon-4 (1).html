Why Forza Horizon 4 Is My Top Game

Forza Horizon 4 combines the thrill of racing with the beauty of an open world set in Britain. The dynamic seasons system keeps the gameplay fresh and exciting.

What Makes It Special

  • Stunning graphics and realistic car physics
  • Dynamic weather and seasonal changes
  • Huge variety of cars to collect and customize
  • Beautiful recreation of British landscapes
  • Perfect balance of arcade and simulation racing
  • Great online multiplayer community

Favorite Features

  • Seasonal championships that change the entire map
  • Photo mode for capturing amazing shots
  • Auction house for trading rare cars
  • Custom livery editor for personalizing cars

Hours Played

I've spent countless hours exploring every corner of the map, collecting cars, and perfecting my racing lines. It's the kind of game I can always come back to for a quick race or a long exploration session.
